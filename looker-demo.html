Key Findings
CP flag
CP and Aramex DIFOT (92% and 91%) are both below the 95% Tier 1 benchmark. With 33% of volume on CP, closing the gap to 95% would recover an estimated 130 consignments/quarter from late delivery.
CP pickup
CP pickup on-time (CIFOT) of 87% is below the 90% flag threshold. Late pickups cascade into late deliveries. Review manifest cutoff time with CP account manager.
AusPost
AusPost leading at 97% DIFOT and 99% first-attempt rate. Cost premium vs CP and Aramex is partially offset by lower redelivery costs and fewer customer service contacts.
Metric definitions: DIFOT = Delivered In Full On Time. CIFOT = Collected In Full On Time (pickup SLA). First attempt = no redelivery required. Source: PRODUCT_PRD.CORTEX.CORTEX_ORDERS (AU domestic, 12-month rolling window).
