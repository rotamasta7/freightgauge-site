The problem

Shipping costs are the last thing most businesses actually look at

Dense invoices. Opaque billing. Surcharges that compound quietly. Most businesses never question what they're actually paying.

01

Carrier invoices are opaque

Fuel levies, remote area surcharges, dimensional weight. The invoice total hides all of it.

02

Free shipping isn't free

Every absorbed freight cost is margin leaving the business. Most owners don't know the actual number.

03

Packaging drives more cost than you think

Carriers bill on volumetric weight. An oversized box costs more than the product warrants.

04

No benchmark, no visibility

Without data, there's nothing to negotiate with. FreightGauge gives you the picture.

Is it right for you?

FreightGauge works best if...

  • You ship 50 or more parcels a month with at least one domestic carrier
  • You pay your own freight costs directly, not managed through a 3PL
  • You have access to your carrier invoices or a shipment export
  • You want to know exactly where your freight spend is going
