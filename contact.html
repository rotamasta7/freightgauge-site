Is FreightGauge right for your business?

FreightGauge works best for Australian ecommerce businesses that:

  • Ship at least 50–100 parcels per month
  • Use AusPost, Allied Express, Sendle, or a similar domestic carrier
  • Have access to a carrier invoice in CSV or Excel format
  • Want to understand their freight spend before approaching a carrier for a rate review
  • Are growing and starting to feel the weight of shipping costs on margins

Not sure if it's right for you? Email us anyway — happy to give you an honest answer before any money changes hands.
