Action plan
10 ranked recommendations
Click any card to expand the finding and next steps.
1
Right-size packaging on top 12 SKUs
$370/mo Medium effort
71% of consignments on volumetric billing. Top 12 SKUs are over-packaged — dead weight 0.68 kg, chargeable 1.24 kg. Addressing these covers 64% of total volumetric exposure.
Audit carton dims for top 12 SKUs against AusPost cubic weight calculator
Target chargeable weight within 15% of dead weight
Get cubic weight report from AusPost/CP before ordering new cartons
2
Reduce AusPost Express usage — move to Economy
$340/mo Low effort
42% of AusPost shipments are Express. 61% of those were placed before 2pm on weekdays — Economy service is sufficient. No evidence customers are selecting Express at checkout.
Default non-metro domestic to AusPost Economy where no express preference exists
Offer Express as a paid checkout upgrade at ~$4.50
3
Route additional standard domestic volume to Couriers Please
$310/mo Low effort
Couriers Please is $3.40/parcel cheaper and has equivalent coverage for standard domestic B2C zones. Currently underutilised at 33% of volume. Shifting an additional 15% from AusPost yields $310/mo with no service impact.
Review zone/postcode overlap between AusPost standard and CP network
Update platform routing rules to allocate standard domestic to CP first
4
Raise free shipping threshold from $75 to $90
$290/mo Low effort
$75 threshold means 68% of orders are subsidised. Raising to $90 cuts that to ~52%, saving $290/mo while affecting only low-value order tail.
Update threshold in your ecommerce platform
Add cart progress bar ("$X to free shipping") to offset any abandonment
Monitor conversion for 30 days; rollback trigger at >2% drop
5
Recover remote area surcharges at checkout
$580 one-off Low effort
$1,540 in remote area charges over 3 months. 31 of 47 affected postcodes are on AusPost/CP's published remote list — predictable at checkout. Pass-through or pre-screen to recover.
Cross-reference destination postcodes against carrier remote area lists (updated quarterly)
Add remote area surcharge line item at checkout via postcode lookup
