What gets analysed

Our AI analysis engine works from your carrier invoice, shipment records, and order data to build a consignment-level model of your entire freight spend.

01

Cost breakdown

Base charge, fuel surcharge, and all other surcharges — separated, quantified, and benchmarked as a percentage of total spend.

02

Weight and volumetric analysis

Dead weight vs. volumetric weight vs. chargeable weight — per consignment. Flags where dimensional billing is driving cost.

03

Service and carrier mix

How your volume splits across service tiers, carriers, and weight bands — and where you are over-servicing (Express where Standard would do).

04

Geographic spend profile

Cost by state and postcode. Identifies expensive delivery zones that may warrant a free shipping threshold adjustment or zone surcharge review.

05

Shipping margin

What you charged customers for shipping vs. what it cost — per order, in aggregate, and as a percentage of total order value.

06

Outlier identification

Consignments with abnormally high costs, unexpected surcharges, or anomalous weight billing — flagged individually for review.

What we need from you

You do not need to clean or reformat anything. We provide step-by-step export guides for every major platform — just send the raw files.

📄

Carrier invoice  Required

CSV or Excel from AusPost, Couriers Please, Allied Express, or similar. Ideally 3+ months — one month minimum.

📦

Shipment records  Recommended

Article-level export from Starshippit, Machship, or similar — dimensions and dead weight per carton. Required for volumetric analysis. Missing data is noted in the output.

🛒

Orders export  Optional

CSV from Shopify, WooCommerce, or similar. Used to calculate shipping margin, AOV, and conversion analysis. Standard and Premium tiers only.

Common questions

Which tier is right for me?

A rough guide: under $3k/month on freight, start with Lite. Spending $3k to $15k/month and want the full picture, choose Standard. Above $15k/month or ready to start negotiating with your carrier, go Premium. Not sure? Get in touch and we will tell you honestly which one makes sense.

How do I send my data?

Via email after you get in touch. We confirm exactly what we need, you send the files directly. No upload portal, no login. All data is handled securely and used only for your analysis.

What if I do not have dimension data?

The analysis still runs. We note the gap and flag what cannot be calculated — typically the volumetric weight and chargeable weight sections. We will tell you exactly what you are missing out on, which is often useful in itself.

Which carriers do you work with?

Any Australian domestic carrier — AusPost, Allied Express, Couriers Please, TNT, StarTrack, and others. If you use multiple carriers, we can analyse all of them together.

What does AI-powered mean in practice?

FreightGauge uses AI to process, clean, and analyse your freight data at a consignment and article level — tasks that would take a human analyst days to do manually. The AI identifies patterns, flags outliers, calculates volumetric weight across every consignment, and surfaces findings ranked by financial impact. Every output is reviewed and validated before delivery.

Is GST included in the prices?

No — all prices shown are ex-GST. FreightGauge is not currently GST registered, so no GST applies at this time.

What is not included at any tier?

Carrier contract negotiation on your behalf, platform integrations or API work, implementation of recommendations, and ongoing monitoring or retainer-based reporting. Need something outside this scope? Get in touch.
