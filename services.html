What gets analysed

Built from your invoice, shipment, and order data. Consignment-level — nothing gets averaged out.

01

Cost breakdown

Base charge, fuel surcharge, and all other surcharges. Separated, quantified, and benchmarked as a share of total spend.

02

Weight and volumetric analysis

Dead weight vs. volumetric weight vs. chargeable weight, per consignment. Flags where dimensional billing is quietly driving up costs.

03

Service and carrier mix

How your volume splits across service tiers, carriers, and weight bands. Includes where you're over-servicing, like paying for Express when Standard would do.

04

Geographic spend profile

Cost by state and postcode. Identifies expensive delivery zones that may need a threshold adjustment or zone surcharge review.

05

Shipping margin

What you charged customers vs. what it actually cost. Per order, in aggregate, and as a share of total order value.

06

Outlier identification

Consignments with abnormally high costs, unexpected surcharges, or anomalous weight billing. Each one flagged for review.

What I need from you

You don't need to clean or reformat anything. I'll send step-by-step export guides. Just send the raw files.

📄

Carrier invoice  Required

CSV or Excel from AusPost, Couriers Please, Allied Express, or similar. Ideally 3 months or more. One month is the minimum.

📦

Shipment records  Recommended

Article-level export from Starshippit, Machship, or similar. Dimensions and dead weight per carton. Without this, the volumetric analysis can't run and I'll flag exactly what's missing.

🛒

Orders export  Optional

CSV from Shopify, WooCommerce, or similar. Used to calculate shipping margin, AOV, and conversion analysis. Standard and Premium tiers only.

Export guides available for: AusPost MyPost Business  ·  Shopify  ·  Starshippit  ·  Machship  ·  WooCommerce  ·  BigCommerce

Common questions

Which tier is right for me?

Under $3k/month on freight: Lite. Between $3k and $15k and want the full picture: Standard. Over $15k/month, or you want to go straight to your carrier with a rate negotiation: Premium. Still not sure? Get in touch and I'll tell you honestly.

How do I send my data?

Via email once you've kicked things off. I'll confirm exactly what files are needed, then you send them over. No portal, no login. Your data is used only for your analysis.

What if I don't have dimension data?

The analysis still runs. I flag what can't be calculated, typically the volumetric and chargeable weight sections. Knowing what's missing is useful on its own.

Which carriers do you work with?

Any Australian domestic carrier. AusPost, Allied Express, Couriers Please, TNT, StarTrack, and others. Multiple carriers? I'll analyse all of them together.

What does AI-powered mean in practice?

It processes and analyses your freight data at a consignment and article level. That's work that would take a human analyst a couple of days. It flags outliers, calculates volumetric weight across every shipment, and ranks findings by financial impact. Everything gets reviewed before it goes out.

Is GST included in the prices?

No. All prices are ex-GST. As a business below the GST registration threshold, no GST applies to your invoice.

Can I see a sample report before committing?

Yes. See the sample report on this page or download the PDF directly. No commitment required.

What's not included?

Carrier negotiation on your behalf, platform integrations, implementation of recommendations, and ongoing retainer work. All out of scope. Need something different? Get in touch.
