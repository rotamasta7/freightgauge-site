Why FreightGauge exists

Most small ecommerce businesses send thousands of parcels a year and have no real idea where the money goes. They get an invoice, pay it, and move on. Pulling it apart feels hard, and there's no obvious person to ask.

Freight cost analysis isn't complicated. But it does require knowing what to look for, and processing a lot of data consistently. FreightGauge combines consistent, consignment-level freight analysis with hands-on logistics expertise: the analysis processes every consignment at scale, the human expertise validates findings and frames recommendations that are actually actionable.

FreightGauge exists to make that analysis accessible. A fixed-price freight health check with clear findings and a practical action plan, delivered to business owners who have better things to do than spend a week building pivot tables.

"Most SMB merchants are overpaying on freight and have no clear view of where the money goes. That's a fixable problem. FreightGauge is how you find it."

What FreightGauge is and isn't

FreightGauge is a productised service. It has a fixed scope, a fixed price, and a fixed turnaround. It's designed to be fast, independent, and useful. Not comprehensive consulting.

It won't negotiate your carrier contract for you, implement a new shipping strategy, or replace your 3PL. What it will do is give you the data and direction you need to have those conversations from a position of knowledge rather than guesswork.
